Niagra Falls

Geographic Location: North America

Niagara Falls is located on the border between Canada and the United States. Connecting Lake Erie and Lake Ontario, Niagara Falls has the highest flow rate of any waterfall in North America.

Niagara Falls draws in thousands of visitors every year, and for good reason. As such, there are numerous things that you can see while you visit the falls, and it is very easy to make a full trip out of seeing this wonderful sight.

Boulder, Colorado

Geographic Location: North America

Boulder Colorado is located in the United States. It is located at the base of the Rocky Mountains, and is home to the University of Colorado Boulder.

Boulder Colorado is a great place to visit if you are looking for a place to hike, ski, or just enjoy the outdoors. It is also a great place to visit if you are looking for a place to eat, as it has a wide variety of restaurants to choose from.

New York City, New York

Geographic Location: North America

New York City is located in the United States. It is located on the east coast, and is home to the Statue of Liberty, the Empire State Building, and Times Square.

New York City is a great place to visit if you are looking for a place to shop, eat, or just enjoy the city. It is also a great place to visit if you are looking for a place to see a show, as it has a wide variety of shows to choose from.
